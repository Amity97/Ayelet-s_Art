About

I look for the colors in life.

Hi, I'm Ayelet Alpert Yeruham. After a full career in law enforcement, I returned to the place that was always home for me: drawing, painting, and looking closely at the world.

Art has been with me since I was young. It was my refuge, my language, and my way of turning emotions, memories, dreams, and everyday moments into something visible.

I work with oil, acrylic, pastel, pencil, charcoal, and watercolor. My paintings move between inner landscapes and the world around me: city scenes, animals, portraits, and works on paper that carry the feeling of a moment.
