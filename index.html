About Me

Hey there! I'm Ayelet Alpert Yeruham, a passionate artist diving into the world of creativity and expression. After a fulfilling career in law enforcement, I've embraced my true calling as an artist. My journey with art began long before my days in uniform. From a young age, I found refuge and inspiration in the strokes of a paintbrush and the flow of pencil on paper. Over the past years, I've honed my skills and developed a unique style of my own. I strive to capture the essence of my imagination and experiences on canvas.
